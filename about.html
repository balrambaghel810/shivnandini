About

Shivnandani Creations By Manisha Sharma

Shivnandani Creations was born from a love for hand woven textiles, delicate detailing and soft, feminine colour stories. Each saree, suit, dupatta, fabric and bedding piece is carefully curated to feel luxurious yet effortless — the kind of pieces you reach for again and again.

Blending traditional weaving inspirations with a modern, pastel-first palette, the label focuses on silhouettes that drape beautifully, feel light on the skin and transition seamlessly from day to occasion wear.

Every collection is designed under the eye of Manisha Sharma, keeping real women and their everyday lives in mind — from intimate family celebrations to milestone moments.

Philosophy

To create pieces that feel like an extension of you — gentle, elevated and quietly statement-making.

Palette

Creams, blush pinks, beiges and soft neutrals that flatter every skin tone and photograph beautifully.
